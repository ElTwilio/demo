Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment? +

You can start investing with as little as $10 for most products. IRA accounts require a minimum of $1,000.

How do I get my money back? +

When a loan is repaid, your principal plus interest is returned to your Groundfloor account. You can then withdraw it or reinvest in other loans.

What are the risks? +

All investments carry risk. Real estate loans can default, and you may lose some or all of your investment. We recommend diversifying across multiple loans to manage risk.

How long are the loan terms? +

Most loans have terms of 6-12 months, though some may be shorter or longer depending on the project.
