Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Groundfloor

How does Groundfloor work? +
Groundfloor lets you invest in short-term real estate loans individually or through our diversified Flywheel Portfolio. You fund loans, earn interest as borrowers repay, and get your principal back when projects complete.
What is the minimum investment? +
The minimum investment is $100 for the Flywheel Portfolio, $10 for individual Loans (LROs), and $1,000 for Notes. This makes real estate investing accessible to everyone.
What returns can I expect? +
The Flywheel Portfolio has delivered an average 11.5% return since launch. Individual loans offer rates from 5% to 15%, and Notes provide 5-7% returns. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.
What are the risks? +
Like all investments, Groundfloor carries risk. Borrowers may default, and you could lose some or all of your investment. However, each loan is backed by real property, and diversification through Flywheel helps minimize risk.
How long is my money invested? +
Most loans mature within 6-24 months. The Flywheel Portfolio is designed as a 36-month investment, though you'll receive regular repayments along the way. Notes have fixed terms of 30, 90, or 360 days.
Can I withdraw my money? +
You can withdraw funds as loans are repaid. With Flywheel, you can turn off auto-reinvesting and withdraw repayments. Individual loans and Notes return your principal at maturity.
